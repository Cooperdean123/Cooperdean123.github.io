Grandmas Lemon Meringue Pie

One 9 Inch Pie

30 mins prep time

10 mins cook time

40 mins total

8 servings

INGREIDENTS

One Cup White Sugar

2 tbsp all porpus flour

3 tbsp cornstartch

1/4 tbsp salt

1 1/2 cup water

2 lemons juiced and zested

2bsp butter

4 egg yolks, beaten

1 9 inch pie crust baked

4 egg whites

6 tbsp white sugar

DIRECTIONS

1. preheat oven to 350 degrees

2. Make lemon filling in a medium saucepan

  • whisk together 1 cup sugar flour cornstartch and salt
  • stir in water lemon juice and lemon zest
  • cook over medium high heat stirring frequently, until mixture comes to boil
  • stir in butter
  • place egg yolks in a small bowl, and graudaly whisk in 1/2 cup of hot sugar mixture
  • whisk egg yolk mixture back into remaing sugar mixture
  • bring to a boil and countiune to cook while stirring frequently until thick
  • remove from heat
  • pour filling into baked pastrey shell

3. Make Meringue in a large glass or metal bowl

  • whisk egg whites until fomey
  • add sugar gradually and countine to whip untill sniff peaks form
  • spread Meringue over pie sealing edges of the crust

    • 4. Bake for 10 minutes or untill Meringue is golden brown
